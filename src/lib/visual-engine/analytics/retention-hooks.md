# LakuAI: Daily Loop Retention Hooks

This document outlines the heuristic hooks used to drive daily merchant engagement and habit formation.

## 1. The "Morning Refresh" Hook
- **Trigger**: 7 days since the last generation for a specific product.
- **Message**: "Trend Alert: Your Shopee Hero image might need a refresh. New 'Soft Daylight' aesthetics are trending in your category."
- **Action**: One-click "Refresh Listing" using existing SPIO.

## 2. The "Campaign Readiness" Hook
- **Trigger**: 14 days before a major Malaysian campaign (Raya, 11.11, etc.).
- **Message**: "Raya is coming! 🌙 Transform your current listings into festive versions with one click."
- **Action**: Bulk apply "Raya Campaign" layer to existing projects.

## 3. The "Mobile Performance" Hook
- **Trigger**: High regeneration rate detected on a specific image type.
- **Message**: "We noticed you're fine-tuning this shot. Try our 'High-Contrast mobile boost' for better visibility."
- **Action**: Suggest VSO override for better commercial performance.
